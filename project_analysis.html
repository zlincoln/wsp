Project Analysis | HG Skis

Site Intent

The intent of this project is to overhaul the design of hgskis.com. My goal is to deliver a flat UI look and feel with proper HTML5/CSS3. The current site isn't responsive and lacks proper white-space. I intend to implement solutions to fix this, in order to convert more consumers to customers, and deliver a consistent experience across all devices.

Competitive Analysis

The ski industry has many major players and will be hard to penetrate. Our strategy will be to market ourselves in the same way craft-beer brewers do - appeal to a sense of supporting local business & make sure to create a sense of community.
